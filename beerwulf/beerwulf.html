Scottish Newcastle

Newcastle Brown Ale was originally produced by the Tyne Brewery in Newcastle, but is now brewed by John Smiths in North Yorkshire, UK. The famous logo with the blue star dates back to the time of the beers launch in 1930. The five points of the star represent the five breweries in Newcastle. In recent years, the brand made waves with a controversial advertising campaign in the US market. Their motto is no bollocks. Try it and you will understand why.
